About BiharFOSS

Our Mission

To promote Free and Open Source Software culture across Bihar, empowering developers and enthusiasts to contribute to global open source projects.

What We Do

We organize workshops, hackathons, meetups, and conferences to spread awareness about FOSS and help people get started with open source contributions.

Join Us

Whether you're a beginner or an experienced developer, everyone is welcome. Be part of Bihar's growing open source ecosystem.
